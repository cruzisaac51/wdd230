The site For Audience

Assignment Audience page

Target audience

My weather site is made for adults age 24-37 years-old. who knows the importance of this weather site and the importance of how to use technology. My audience do not want loose time need it to be impresive and relax.

Personas

  1. Mayra is 26 years old to organize her day Mayra plans her outdoor activities. Mayra works from home and has a specific schedule in which she can go to the supermarket to buy household items, she needs a website that provides the correct climate to be able to carry out her activities without surprises

  2. Heribay is 30 years old, works as a nurse in a renowned hospital, he needs a website capable of giving him a quick overview of what kind of patients they could work with and have the facilities as prepared as possible.

Scenarios

  1. Mayra has just moved to Puerto Rico and a friend has told her that the "yunke" is very beautiful and a place to spend the weekend in the natural with beautiful landscapes, rivers and waterfalls. She decides to investigate the place and it worries her a little since people complain about how dangerous that place is when it is raining so she has the need to check on the website when is the best day to visit that park

  2. Heribay plans a trip to Chicago to see his favorite football team. His trip will be with his wife and 8-month-old son, so he is a little worried about the weather since his young son cannot be exposed to such cold climates. Heribay's favorite team will show up 3 times during the following year so he needs to see the best date for his trip since he doesn't have the exact date yet but he would like to be able to go and enjoy those days with his family and see his team instead. Favorite for that he will use the best weather website.

  3. Mayra is going to get married on Friday and wants to go on vacation that weekend to enjoy her honeymoon, Mayra has several beaches as options that are Puerto Vallarta, Cancun, and Mazatlan so she needs to be able to find which place will be better Mayra will consult on the weather site and see if it will be and beautiful weather and spend those wonderful days

  4. Heribay has been working in the hospital for 24 hours and it is time to leave, Heribay on this day did not see the sunlight and he is worried about what the weather will be like outside, so he looks at the weather site to see what kind of environmental conditions await him outside and be able to know whether to put on a coat or take out the umbrella to be able to go home to rest

  5. As part of Mayra's routine, she goes for a walk every afternoon on the main avenue of her city, yesterday she went out and was surprised by a very strong electric storm so she was not prepared with any umbrella. when she got home she felt a little sick because of the weather, Mayra really needs a website where she can visualize the weather for the next few days to be able to organize herself so that this does not happen again and to be able to enjoy a pleasant walk and calm
